Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Rim & Face ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application for professional shaft alignment calculations.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Analytics: App features used, calculation frequency (anonymized)
  • Ad-Related Data: Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers for personalized ads

Information You Provide

  • Calculation Data: Machine dimensions and dial readings (stored locally only)
  • App Preferences: Language settings, measurement units
Important: All engineering calculation data is processed and stored locally on your device. No sensitive technical data is transmitted to external servers.

How We Use Information

Primary Uses

  • Provide accurate shaft alignment calculations
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Support multiple languages and technical standards

Data Processing

  • All calculation data is processed locally on your device
  • No sensitive engineering data is transmitted to external servers
  • Anonymous usage statistics help improve app functionality

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Google AdMob

  • We use Google AdMob to display advertisements
  • AdMob may collect and use advertising identifiers
  • For AdMob privacy practices, see: Google Privacy Policy

No Sale of Personal Data

  • We do not sell, trade, or transfer personal information
  • Engineering calculation data remains private and local
  • No sharing of technical measurement data with third parties

Data Security

  • Calculation data is stored locally on your device using secure methods
  • App uses industry-standard security measures
  • No sensitive technical data is transmitted over networks
  • Regular security updates through app store

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Calculation history can be cleared within the app
  • Advertising preferences can be managed through device settings
  • App can be uninstalled to remove all local data
  • Language and measurement preferences are user-controlled

Opt-Out Options

  • Disable personalized ads through device settings
  • Contact us for data-related requests
  • Use airplane mode for offline calculations

International Data Transfers

As the app supports 7 languages (English, Hindi, Chinese, Arabic, Spanish, French, German), we design our privacy practices to comply with international standards including GDPR and similar regulations.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for professional engineering use and industrial applications. It is not directed at children under 13 years of age.

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes. Material changes will be highlighted in app update notifications.
